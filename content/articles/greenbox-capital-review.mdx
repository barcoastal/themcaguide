Greenbox Capital is a Miami-based MCA funder operating since 2012. They advertise some of the lowest starting factor rates in the industry at 1.09, though qualifying for that rate is uncommon.

<h2 id="key-terms">Key Terms and Rates</h2>

- **Factor Rates:** 1.09 to 1.40+ (starting rate rarely achieved)
- **Funding:** Up to $500,000
- **Repayment:** 3 to 18 months
- **Funding Speed:** 1-2 business days
- **Minimum Revenue:** $7,500/month ($90K annual)
- **Time in Business:** 9 months
- **Minimum Credit Score:** 500

<h2 id="pros">Pros</h2>

- Low minimum revenue requirement
- Advertised starting factor rates below competitors
- Accepts credit scores as low as 500
- Fast decision turnaround

<h2 id="cons">Cons</h2>

- Most borrowers don't qualify for the 1.09 floor rate
- Several reviews mention pushy sales and follow-up calls
- Fee structure not always clearly disclosed until late in process
- Renewal rates sometimes higher than initial advance
