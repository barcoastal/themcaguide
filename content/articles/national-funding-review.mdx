National Funding has been operating since 1999 and has funded over $4.5 billion to 75,000+ small businesses. Based in San Diego, they offer MCAs, short-term loans, and equipment financing. Here is what you need to know.

<h2 id="key-terms-and-rates">Key Terms and Rates</h2>

- **Factor Rates:** 1.10 to 1.45
- **Funding Amounts:** $5,000 to $500,000 (MCA); up to $150,000 (equipment)
- **Repayment Terms:** 4 to 15 months (MCA)
- **Funding Speed:** Same day to 3 business days
- **Minimum Credit Score:** 575
- **Minimum Annual Revenue:** $250,000
- **Time in Business:** 6 months minimum

<h2 id="pros">Pros</h2>

- **Dedicated funding specialist** for each applicant
- **Same-day funding** possible for approved borrowers
- **Equipment financing option** in addition to MCA
- **Since 1999** — longest operating history among mid-sized MCA funders

<h2 id="cons">Cons</h2>

- **Higher revenue minimum** ($250K) excludes smaller businesses
- **Factor rates climb to 1.45** for higher-risk profiles
- **Some borrowers report higher rates** than initial estimates after underwriting
