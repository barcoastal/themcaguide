If you need capital today or tomorrow, a handful of MCA lenders actually deliver on same-day funding promises. But the reality is more nuanced than the marketing claims. This guide covers who funds fastest, what it actually costs, and how to prepare your application for the fastest possible turnaround.

<h2 id="fastest-mca-lenders">Fastest MCA Lenders in 2026</h2>

**1. Rapid Finance** — Same-day funding possible for approved borrowers with clean documentation. Often the fastest, but transparency concerns warrant careful contract review.

**2. Fundbox** — Instant approval and funding for existing customers. Initial approval takes 1-3 business days; subsequent draws fund same-day.

**3. Greenbox Capital** — Decisions within hours, funding typically next business day (some same-day for qualified borrowers).

**4. National Funding** — Same-day funding possible, longer for larger amounts.

**5. Credibly** — Same-day funding for qualified mid-to-high revenue borrowers.

**6. Shopify Capital, Amazon Lending, PayPal Working Capital** — Minutes to hours for existing platform sellers.

<h2 id="how-to-actually-get-same-day-funding">How to Actually Get Same-Day Funding</h2>

To maximize your chances:

1. **Apply in the morning** (before 10am PT) to ensure your application enters the day's funding queue
2. **Submit clean bank statements** (3 months, no NSFs, consistent deposits)
3. **Have your EIN, tax ID, and business formation docs ready**
4. **Ensure your business bank account is set up for ACH** (same-day funding requires fast ACH)
5. **Apply directly with the funder** — brokers add 1-2 days
6. **Pre-qualify with multiple lenders** simultaneously so you can accept the fastest offer

<h2 id="costs-of-speed">What Same-Day Speed Costs</h2>

Typical premium for same-day vs 2-3 day funding:

- Factor rate often 0.05 to 0.10 higher
- Sometimes an expedited processing fee of $100-$500
- Potentially higher origination fee (2-4% vs standard 0-2%)

On a $50,000 advance, this translates to $2,500-$5,000 extra for same-day speed. If you truly need the money today, that cost may be justified. If you can wait 48 hours, standard funding saves significant money.

<h2 id="alternatives-for-speed">Alternatives for Fast Capital</h2>

- **Business credit cards** — instant use once approved, 0% intro APR possible
- **Platform capital** (Shopify, Amazon, PayPal) — minutes for existing sellers
- **Line of credit draws** (if you have a Fundbox, Kabbage, or BlueVine line) — same-day draws
- **Personal loan** — 1-3 days at most banks for qualified borrowers, often cheaper than MCA
